## VAR Implementation: Controversy and Impact

The 2018 World Cup marked the first time that Video Assistant Referee (VAR) technology was used in the tournament (The Guardian, n.d.)[4]. The use of VAR sparked discussions about the balance between technology and the spirit of the game, with opinions divided among players, coaches, and fans.
